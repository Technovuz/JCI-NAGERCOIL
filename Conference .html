Nagercoil CONFERENCE 2025

Join the 2025 Nagercoil Conference

Every year in spring, the Nagercoil Conference (NLO) is organized in a different Nagercoil city. The official JCI program with meetings for national presidents, training sessions, and workshops is surrounded by company visits, tourist excursions, guest speakers, debating and public speaking competitions, festive evenings, and sports events.
