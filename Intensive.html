Strategic Growth Program

Strategic Growth Program is targeted for Nagercoil JCI organisations in need of structured support. The outcome is a solid program for organisations to be executed to achieve sustainable growth. In collaboration with the organisation, the target is not just doing quick fixes but creating sustainable solutions and developing a continuous improvement culture & problem-solving skills throughout Nagercoil!

Program Content +
  • Understanding the current situation
    • Quick root cause analysis to find what needs to be addressed most urgently
    • Target to find the underlying pain points to be fixed first
  • Creating an action plan
    • What needs to be done to get the actions done, what kind of support is needed
    • What kind of resources are available / needed
  • Finding the correct mentors / sparring partners
    • Learning from the other organizations who have had similar issues / experience on the areas of needs
    • Pairing with the correct mentors to share the knowledge
    • Connecting with the JCI Nagercoil Growth Expert program members
  • Following up the action plan
When should you apply? +

The program really aims at helping National and Local Organizations that face difficulties.

  • The program aims at helping Nagercoil National and Local Organizations that face difficulties.
  • Does your Organization suffer from membership structural decline?
  • Does your national organization have less local organization than the fingers of its National President left hand?
  • Is your National Organization in trouble regarding succession planning?

If you answer yes to 2 or more of those questions, Strategic Growth Program and NLO may be able to help! Apply now!

Applying for the 2025 program +

To apply for 2025 Strategic Growth Program simply fill in the form at “Apply for support!” We will reach out to you shortly after you do it.

Mentoring

With the mentoring program the Nagercoil Development Council (NLO) wants to offer an opportunity for National and Local JCI Organisationsto receive support in their development and an opportunity for experienced members to make a difference and put their know-how to use.

Mentoring in general is a confidential relationship between the mentees and a mentor where more experienced mentors help their mentees to develop and advance throughout their challenges. As part of the Accelerator the mentees will apply as a team of 2-5 people.
