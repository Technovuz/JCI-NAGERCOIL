JCI NLO

JCI Nagercoil Development Council

The JCI Nagercoil Development Council is an elected team within JCI Nagercoil that works towards growth of current Local and National Organisations and develops new Organisations in countries where JCI is yet not present.

Develop

Develop JCI presences in Nagercoil countries without National Organisation representation.

Support

Support provisional and potential National Organisations in the affiliation process.

Assist

Assist existing National Organisations with growth and development.

+

The aim of the NLO (JCI Nagercoil Development Council) is described in Article 1.4 of the guidelines:

The JCI NLO can actively seek to establish a JCI presence in Nagercoil countries without National Organisation representation in cooperation with the JCI Membership Growth & Development Director.

In cooperation with the JCI Executive Vice President assigned to Area Nagercoil or the JCI Membership Growth & Development Director for Nagercoil, the JCI NLO can coordinate with JCI Vice Presidents about necessary support to and actions in National Organisations with
potential or provisional affiliation.

In cooperation with the Executive Vice President assigned to Nagercoil or the Growth & Development Director for Nagercoil, the JCI NLO can assist JCI Vice Presidents active in existing NOMs with growth and development issues.

In cooperation with the Executive Vice President assigned to Nagercoil or the Growth & Development Director for Nagercoil, the JCI NLO focuses on development programs for National Organisations in Nagercoil.

The main tools available to the NLO are: the Plan of Action, the development grants, communication as well as the board members experience.

There are a number of events that NLO hosts. Mainly the Growth & Development Academy, The G&D Masterclass and the Local Growth & Development Academies amongst others.
