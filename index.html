JCI Nagercoil

Leaders for a changing world

JCI Nagercoil is the area representative of JCI (Junior Chamber International), a global nonprofit membership organisation of young active citizens ages 18 to 40. Our purpose is to provide development opportunities that empower young people to take action for positive change.

JCI Nagercoil

The JCI Nagercoil is located in Nagercoil. The permanent, full-time staff provides administrative, communication support, and other services to JCI members in Nagercoil.

JCI Nagercoil Board of Directors

JCI Nagercoil Board of Directors consists of elected members who have the opportunity to hold their leadership positions for one year within the organisation to share their knowledge and talents.
