JCI Europe Headquarters

The JCI Europe Headquarters is located in Berlin, Germany. The permanent, full-time staff provides administrative, communication support, and other services to JCI members in Europe.

JCI Europe Board of Directors

JCI Europe's Board of Directors consists of elected members who have the opportunity to hold their leadership positions for one year within the organisation to share their knowledge and talents.

European Development Council

The JCI European Development Council is an elected body within JCI Europe and works towards the growth of Local and National Organisations and develops new Organisations in countries where JCI is still not present.
