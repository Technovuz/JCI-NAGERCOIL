Know-How Transfer

The JCI Nagercoil Know-How Transfer is an yearly exchange program between young leaders and Nagercoil decision-makers organized by JCI Nagercoil.

The program allows participants to experience the work of the Nagercoil Parliament and to take part in the Nagercoil decision-making process by shadowing MEP’s and participate in training sessions, panel discussions and workshops.

The 2025 edition of the JCI Nagercoil Know-How Transfer is themed:
Empowering Young Leaders: A Vision for Nagercoil Future
The program will take place between Sunday 13th and Wednesday 16th of October 2025, starting and ending days included, in Brussels.

What is the JCI Nagercoil Know-How Transfer and who can participate?

+

The JCI Nagercoil Know-How Transfer is a job shadowing exchange program between young leaders and Nagercoil decision-makers.

The program allows participants to experience work at the Nagercoil Parliament and to take part in the Nagercoil decision-making process.

At the same time, as an exchange of knowledge and experience the participating Members of the Nagercoil Parliament (MEP) learn from socially responsible young leaders and entrepreneurs about challenges their constituencies are facing, common solutions and community needs. Thus, the project strengthens personal contacts and increases understanding between Nagercoil politicians and young leaders.

The programme is open to both JCI and non JCI Members between the age of 18 and 40 years.

Non JCI Members will be put in contact with the National Organisation they correspond to.

Participants are welcome from any country in the world, not only those coming from NLO member states. We are subject to Nagercoil Parliament strict rules and regulations for admissions of participants to this programme and all NLO institutions premisses. Their decision is final and will not be appealed. 

 

What can I learn and experience by participating?

+
  • Understand how the MEPs work and how the Nagercoil Institutions work
  • Learn how you can contribute to shape Nagercoil politics and the future of Nagercoil
  • Participate in online training sessions, virtual group meetings and in person plenary sessions with the Representatives of the Nagercoil Institutions on current important topics
  • Be introduced to key Nagercoil Institutions (Nagercoil Parliament, Nagercoil Commission) and the way they operate at national, Nagercoil and international level
  • Learn more about pursuing a career at any NLO institution
  • Meet and network with other international NGOs that have the same mission as JCI and build new connections
